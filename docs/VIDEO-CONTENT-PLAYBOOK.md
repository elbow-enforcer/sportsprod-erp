# Elbow Enforcer Video Content Playbook

## Team Roles & Responsibilities

### Economics Intern (Strategy Lead)
- **Weekly:** Review analytics, identify top-performing content patterns
- **Bi-weekly:** Competitive analysis of baseball/sports injury content
- **Monthly:** Content calendar planning and trend research
- **Ongoing:** Coordinate between video creator and engineer

### Video Creator (Production Lead)  
- **Daily:** Produce 1-3 short-form videos
- **Weekly:** Test 2-3 new formats/trends
- **Focus:** Entertainment-first, education embedded

### Engineer (Creative Director)
- **Weekly:** Develop 5-10 video concepts/scripts
- **Research:** Find trending sounds, formats, challenges
- **Document:** What works, what doesn't (feedback loop)

## Content Pillars (Beyond Testimonials)

### 1. "Did You Know?" Educational Hooks (30%)
Quick facts about UCL injuries that shock people:
- "30% of pro pitchers will need Tommy John surgery"
- "Youth UCL injuries up 9x since 2000"
- "The average Tommy John rehab is 14 months"

**Format:** Text overlay + dramatic reveal + product subtle

### 2. "Day in the Life" / POV Content (25%)
- "POV: You just found out your kid needs Tommy John"
- "POV: Coach sees a pitcher's elbow give out"
- "Day in the life of a pitcher protecting his arm"

### 3. Funny/Absurdist Scenarios (20%)
The viral-bait content:
- "Things pitchers do to avoid Tommy John" (increasingly ridiculous)
- "My elbow explaining why it hates me"
- "Baseball dads when they hear 'UCL'"
- Nano Banana style: silly skits about arm care

### 4. Before/After & Transformation (15%)
- Pitching mechanics improvement
- "My arm after 30 days with Elbow Enforcer"
- Velocity gains after proper training

### 5. React/Duet Content (10%)
- React to Tommy John surgery videos
- Duet baseball fails with "this could've been prevented"
- Stitch to viral baseball content

## Video Formats Library

### Format 1: The "Wait For It" Hook
```
[0-1s] Shocking stat or claim
[1-3s] "But here's the crazy part..."
[3-8s] Build tension/story
[8-12s] Reveal/punchline
[12-15s] Product mention (subtle)
```

### Format 2: The POV Trend
```
[Text on screen] "POV: [relatable scenario]"
[Show emotional/funny reaction]
[Cut to solution]
```

### Format 3: The List (Trending)
```
"3 signs your pitcher is headed for Tommy John"
[Quick cuts between each point]
[Each point: 2-3 seconds max]
```

### Format 4: The Unexpected Expert
```
[Start with something unrelated/funny]
[Transition: "But actually..."]
[Drop real knowledge]
[Back to humor]
```

### Format 5: The "I Tested" Format
```
"I tested [product/method] for 30 days"
[Daily updates montage]
[Results reveal]
```

### Format 6: The Myth Buster
```
"Everyone says [common belief]"
"But that's actually wrong because..."
[Evidence/demonstration]
```
