## Executive Summary

Comprehensive go-to-market strategy for **Elbow Enforcer**, a patented sports medicine device focused on UCL (ulnar collateral ligament) injury prevention and rehabilitation for baseball players. The device addresses the growing epidemic of Tommy John surgeries across youth, amateur, and professional baseball.

**Key Product Details:**
- Price Point: ~$1,000/unit
- First Batch: 1,000 units
- Tooling Investment: $100-120K
- Existing Assets: elbowenforcer.com, ~4,000 email subscribers, @elbowenforcer Instagram, granted patent

**Market Opportunity:**
- Skyrocketing UCL injuries in baseball (youth to pro)
- Tommy John surgery epidemic with no device-based prevention at scale
- Comparable adoption trajectory: Iron Neck

## Growth Loops
*Reference: referral-program, free-tool-strategy skills*

### Referral Program Design

**Structure: Double-Sided Reward**

| Participant | Reward |
|-------------|--------|
| Referrer | $100 credit toward next purchase OR $50 cash |
| Referee | $100 off first purchase |

**Why This Works:**
- High LTV ($999) justifies generous reward
- Parents naturally share injury prevention tips
- PTs refer patients regularly

**Referral Mechanics:**
- Unique referral link per customer
- Trackable discount codes (FRIEND-[NAME])
- Automated reward payout after 30-day return window

**Viral Triggers:**
- After purchase: "Know another baseball parent? Give them $100 off"
- After positive NPS: "Loved Elbow Enforcer? Share the protection"
- On social: Shareable results cards ("Protected for X throws")

### PT Partner Program (Affiliate)

**Commission Structure:**
- 15% commission on referred sales ($150/unit)
- Recurring for 12 months if subscription added
- Tiered: 20% after 10 referrals

**PT Enablement Kit:**
- Product samples for clinic display
- Patient brochures and handouts
- Referral tracking dashboard
- CE credit materials (if developed)

### Free Tool Strategy

**Tool 1: UCL Injury Risk Calculator**
- Input: Age, pitch count, throwing history, pain history
- Output: Risk score + personalized prevention recommendations
- Lead capture: Email required for full report
- SEO target: "UCL injury risk assessment," "Tommy John risk calculator"

**Tool 2: Tommy John Surgery Cost Calculator**
- Input: Player age, career goals, insurance status
- Output: Estimated total cost (surgery, rehab, lost time)
- Lead capture: Email for detailed breakdown
- SEO target: "Tommy John surgery cost," "UCL surgery recovery time"

**Tool 3: Pitch Count Tracker (Mobile-First)**
- Simple pitch tracking app/web tool
- Weekly UCL load recommendations
- Brand exposure, data collection opportunity
- Can be ungated (pure brand play)

**Build Recommendation:**
- Start with UCL Risk Calculator (highest lead gen potential)
- Use no-code tool (Outgrow or Typeform) for MVP
- Estimated leads: 200-500/month with SEO traction
