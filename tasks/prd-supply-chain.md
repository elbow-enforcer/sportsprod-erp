# PRD: Supply Chain & Procurement Module

## Overview
Manage suppliers, purchase orders, and receiving for sportsprod-erp manufacturing.

## User Stories

### Phase 1: Supplier Management
- US-1.1: Supplier list with CRUD
- US-1.2: Supplier detail (contact, terms, lead times)
- US-1.3: Supplier performance metrics

### Phase 2: Purchase Orders
- US-2.1: Create PO from raw material needs
- US-2.2: PO approval workflow
- US-2.3: PO list and status tracking
- US-2.4: PO detail view

### Phase 3: Receiving
- US-3.1: Receive against PO
- US-3.2: Quantity/quality inspection
- US-3.3: Auto-update inventory on receive
- US-3.4: Receiving history

### Phase 4: Routes & Nav
- US-4.1: Routes for /supply-chain/*
- US-4.2: Sidebar navigation
- US-4.3: Permissions (view:supply-chain, edit:supply-chain)
